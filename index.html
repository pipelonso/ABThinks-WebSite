Hello, let me introduce myself. My name is Pipe, and I am the lead developer of the LittleRegress datapack for Minecraft Java. This pack adds a set of elements that contribute to a better role-playing experience as a baby and caregiver within Minecraft. It is dedicated to the ABDL, DL, AB, TB, and TBDL communities, seeking acceptance from anyone regardless of their gender, skin color, place of origin, or culture.

I have made an effort to understand various segments of the community, and it took me quite a long time to reach the conclusion of writing this message and having it appear on the main page of the website.

I have joined several communities exclusively to understand their functioning. I have also read numerous books on Wattpad; some are very nice, even if their writing may not be the best in the world. Nonetheless, I love to see people's experiences with what they do and what they manage to learn. Each person is a different and new universe to learn from and try to understand. I've had good conversations and interactions with some people, though with others, it might not have been the best. I know my limits and the extent to which interaction with someone can be detrimental to me.

My focus has been on making the interaction with each user in this community as comfortable and secure as possible within what that user considers normal. I pay attention to what they want to do and ask for opinions on what they would like to include in the datapack content. I would really like to delve deeper into this at some point; perhaps I could create a section on the website specifically for this, as I consider it important for each person to understand.

Recently, I told my mother about the things I do and why I am part of this community. I tried to show her the positive side of things, but she didn't even want to understand the reason why I joined this community in the first place. Maybe I haven't been officially diagnosed by a psychologist with anxiety problems, and the mere existence of it really stresses me out. When I'm in AB mode, somehow I feel that everything is okay because I simulate the sweet and beautiful ignorance of a baby when nothing was more important than exploring and discovering your surroundings without worrying about anything, as you didn't even know that something bad could happen. I know these activities are not necessarily the cleanest in the world, and I understand that they may be unpleasant for some people, if not many. Some things are even unpleasant for me still. Perhaps I haven't fully adapted to the community yet, but I am learning and understanding little by little.

I also know that many people see this as a sexual fetish, and I understand why they perceive it that way. I know how it feels down there when I wear a diaper. I am an adult who has just emerged from adolescence and experienced many things, so I can say that diapers lend themselves to many unpleasant things, especially for adults. One of the reasons I prefer to avoid such actions at all costs is precisely because when I end up doing something like that, instead of generating the relaxing effect it should, it has the opposite effect and causes more anxiety.

I would also like, for example, to add my own personal touch to the datapack, something that symbolizes in some way the reason why I ended up joining this community. I've been thinking a lot about it because I don't even know if it can make me feel better or worse.

When I started the development of LittleRegress, I hadn't delved so deeply into the community. In fact, it was initially a project just to play with a friend. However, on the day I decided to expand the project and showcase what I was creating on a Discord server where several people became interested in the project, I decided to make the project public and open source because I want each person to be free to choose and do what they like.

I will only take care of the safe side of everything, while also implementing elements that enhance the security of the datapack for different types of audiences.

Perhaps in the future, if it doesn't already exist, I could create a special section to showcase everything I have found out and display all the work behind the development of LittleRegress.

LITTLEREGRESS

LittleRegress en un datapack para minecraft que añade el rol de bebé y cuidador a tu experiencia de minecraft, teniendo en cuenta la seguridad del usuario en un entorno de un solo jugador y multijugador, explorando experiencias aptas para la terapia de regresion, uso por la comunidad ABDL y todas sus variantes, ademas de ser de codigo abierto y totalmente modificable por el usuario.

El datapack actualmente ofrece la posibilidad de tener el rol de bebé y usar elementos creados especificamente para ese rol, como lo son los pañales, ropa, cuna, cambiador, bañera, inodoro, chupon de bebe, silla de bebe y demas.

El datapack se encuentra en desarrollo por lo que el contenido puede cambiar.

LittleRegress is a datapack for Minecraft that adds the baby and caregiver roles to your Minecraft experience, taking into account user safety in both single-player and multiplayer environments. It explores experiences suitable for regression therapy, catering to the ABDL (Adult Baby Diaper Lover) community and all its variants. Additionally, it is open-source and fully customizable by the user.

The current datapack provides the opportunity to take on the role of a baby and use items specifically created for that role, such as diapers, clothing, crib, changing table, bathtub, toilet, baby pacifier, baby chair, and more.

Please note that the datapack is still in development, so the content may undergo changes.
